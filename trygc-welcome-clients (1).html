How your campaign runs

From brief to report,
six client-facing phases.

Behind the scenes it's an 18-stage workflow. On your side, here's what you see and when.

Phase 01 · Intake
Brief + Kick-Off
We collect brand, target, platforms, deliverables, dates, tags. If anything's missing, we flag it immediately — we don't launch with gaps.
Phase 02 · Setup
Influencer List + Approvals
We build a qualified list, you review and approve. Replacements identified for any rejections before we send invitations.
Phase 03 · Outreach
Invitations + Confirmations
Invitations go out, reminders follow, confirmed list locked. You get a status view — not just a number.
Phase 04 · Live
Execution + Logistics
Visit or delivery scheduled, executed, documented. Live issues escalated to you immediately if decisions are needed.
Phase 05 · Coverage
Coverage + QA
Posts collected, archived, QA'd against your brief. Missing coverage worked into active recovery — not forgotten.
Phase 06 · Close
Report + Lessons
Target vs achieved, coverage links, gaps explained, insights logged. Campaign closed with documentation — not a verbal wrap.

Good to know

Fast answers.

How do you vet influencers? +
Every creator is matched against your niche, tier, city, and platform. We validate availability before presenting a list — you approve before any invite goes out. Blocklisted accounts (repeat no-shows, prior coverage failures) are filtered upstream.
What if a creator doesn't post on time? +
We move to Missing Coverage Recovery within 24 hours of SLA breach — structured follow-ups, documented escalation, and if needed, replacement activation. You get a tracker view, not a surprise at report time.
Can you run campaigns in Arabic only? +
Yes. KSA campaigns run Arabic-first (KSA dialect, not formal MSA). Kuwait and UAE are mixed EN/AR depending on creator and audience. Egypt defaults to Arabic with EN support where relevant.
What's included in the final report? +
Target vs achieved (influencer count, posting coverage, deliverables), per-influencer post links, QA pass/fail status, any gaps with documented reasons, lessons learned, and campaign-level insights. Delivered in 24–48 hours of closure.
Who do I contact for escalations? +
Your Account Manager is first line for any decision, change, or concern. For operational escalations (delays, missing coverage beyond SLA), the Ops Lead is copied automatically. You never need to chase multiple people.
